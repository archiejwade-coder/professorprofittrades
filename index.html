What is R?

“R” is a fixed unit of risk per trade.

If your stop-loss is £100 then:

  • +1R = +£100 profit
  • +2R = +£200 profit
  • -1R = -£100 loss

Using R means every trade is measured in the same risk unit, regardless of lot size or pair. The equity curve on this page shows the running total of R over all trades.

This is not account balance, it is strategy performance in risk units.
