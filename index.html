What is R?

R is a fixed unit of risk per trade. It lets you compare results without knowing the account size.

  • If 1R = £100 of risk, then +2R = +£200 profit and -1R = -£100 loss.
  • Every trade on this page is measured in R, so results scale to any account size.
  • The equity curve below is cumulative R across all trades taken.
